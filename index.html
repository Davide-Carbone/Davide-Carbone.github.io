Welcome to my website

I am currently a Fellow in Artificial Intelligence at PRAIRIE-PSAI / PSL University and a postdoctoral researcher at the Laboratoire de Physique de l’ENS (LPENS), working with the Disordered Systems and Applications team. My research sits at the interface of machine learning and statistical mechanics. I develop AI methods to address challenging computational problems in statistical physics, with a particular emphasis on generative modeling and advanced sampling techniques.

Outside of research, I enjoy playing piano and synthesizer in a band, reading science fiction, and dancing for fun.
